About

Mr HABUDUBU, aka ASMO ABDULLAH, is a dedicated Rapper and Lyricist from Bangladesh, now based in Oman. With a focus on powerful storytelling and authentic rap delivery, he brings a raw perspective to the modern hip-hop scene. Every bar he writes is a testament to his journey and his unwavering passion for the art of music.
